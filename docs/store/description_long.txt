# MixMingle — Live Audio Rooms & Social Discovery

## Long Description (Google Play / App Store)

**MixMingle** is the live social platform where real conversations happen. Jump into live audio rooms, discover new people, and build your community — all in one app.

---

### 🎙️ Live Audio Rooms
- Start or join live voice rooms on any topic: music, dating, debate, gaming, and more.
- High-quality Agora-powered audio with audience participation controls.
- React in real time with on-screen emoji bursts and gift animations.

### 🎁 Gifts & Coins
- Send virtual gifts (roses, diamonds, crowns, unicorns…) to your favourite hosts.
- Earn coins by hosting popular rooms and being tipped by fans.
- Spend coins on profile boosts, room boosts, and rare gifts.

### 🔥 Profile & Room Boosts
- Boost your profile to appear at the top of discovery for 24 hours.
- Boost your room to trending for 12 hours and grow your audience fast.

### 💬 Messaging
- One-on-one chat with emoji reactions, image sharing, and read receipts.
- Typing indicators and real-time delivery — like a real conversation.

### ✨ Premium Membership
- Unlock exclusive profile frames, premium badges, and unlimited room creation.
- Remove ads, access analytics, and grow your creator brand.

### 🧭 Discovery
- Swipe through nearby users with shared interests.
- Browse live rooms by genre: chill, hot, music, trending.
- AI-powered "Vibe Match" suggestions updated in real time.

### 🎉 Events
- RSVP to scheduled community events and get calendar reminders.
- Create your own events with ticket links, descriptions, and flyers.

### 🛡️ Safety First
- Robust reporting and moderation tools.
- Block, mute, and report any user with one tap.
- Admin dashboard for platform-level moderation and analytics.
